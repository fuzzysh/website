--- title: steam next fest doesn't suck style: date: 2026-06-17 tags: videogames ---

some steam next fest videogames

i don't like steam next fest! the games are boring! there's never anything cool! it's all slop! asset flips! incremental games! AI junk! deck builders, rogue likes, and deck-building-rogue-likes! what the hell! i don't care!

well, that's not a very reasonable way to think. so i poked my head in to see if i could find anything that spoke to me. of course, i did! there are people out there with pure hearts who are making games they really believe in! these are the ones i believe in.

  • [DekaDuck] an action platformer that reminded me of battletoads
  • [Magical Thief Silky] very erotic and cute!
  • [Cure for Danmaku Synd] a cool concept with really nice art!
  • [The Echo of MeMe-For You Adrift in the Sea of Reveries] an adventure game that seemed very touching
  • [MIHASHIRA] a top down shooter with exagerated weapons and cute girls

DekaDuck - Steam

yep, it's like battletoads! you've got a gun though, and you're a duck. there's a grappling hook too. you can grab enemies or grapple grapple points.

i really like the character designs. dekaduck has a small head and torso with exageratedly huge limbs which is fun, there was also a cute bunny girl. sexy stuff! the enemy designs are also filled with personality. it's a really pleasant game to look at.

playing the game felt pretty awkward. my controller wasn't working, i dunno if the game supports it or not... so i was playing on keyboard. i kept pressing the wrong buttons and never got used to the controls evens after beating the demo. the whole thing just felt awkward.

and while fighting the final boss of the demo... it kept attacking me from off screen! i guess it's my fault for not keeping the boss in my sights... but it still felt bad getting hit by a charging attack i couldn't see coming!

at the end of the demo they asked for feedback, so maybe they know there are some issues. i hope they can tweak it and make something awesome.

Magical Thief Silky - Steam

so moe! it's like megaman. it's yuri!

it's REALLY indulgent. like. boobs swaying back and forth constantly on your screen indulgent. there's a character model overlaid on top of the action gameplay during the entire game, and this model even reacts to your inputs. firing your gun? the model pulls out their gun. using your melee attack? the model follows. if your health gets low she'll even adopt a pained expression and begin panting. it's very erotic.

it's a pretty simple run and gun! the levels were fun, i thought it was gonna be really easy at first but there ended up being some challenging bits! the game seems to have infinite lives so it never got frustrating even when it felt like a section was a little unfair. i knew i could overcome it!

i like the character designs, the story seems cute enough.

through the power of moe a game like this can be elevated to new heights!

also, if one of your characters die you respawn as the other and can revive her with a yurikiss... this game's kinda awesome.

Cure for Danmaku Synd - Steam

a danmaku game inside the human body with a twist! instead of firing bullets as you dodge you have a shield and intentionally take hits in order to trigger a counter attack. the shield has a limit, so carefully managing your shield while dodging and not dodging bullets is the core of the game.

i like the setting! in the future, nanomachines have all but eradicated disease. however, a new disease - danmaku syndrome - now threatens the human race. a disease caused by nanomachines going haywire!? fight as a nanomachine to save a patient who's suffering from danmaku syndrome and uncover the true cause of this new disease!

you play as a cute girl, and she's cute. i like her. the whole game is cute, despite the grotesque setting. there's lots of colours. the whole game has a very unified style. even the menus!

the game offers a really good challenge, i really struggled with the final stage of the demo! but by customizing my kit and not giving up i was able to overcome! it was a great feeling!

oh, the translation was pretty rought though. thankfully it didn't stop me from being able to more or less understand what was being communicated, but watch out for that. at least this means it was translated by a human!...?

The Echo of MeMe-For You Adrift in the Sea of Reverie - Steam

this game is really cute and sweet. not sure what it's about... but it's full of delicate feelings. it's really precious. cute girls too.

i don't have much to say about it. you visit different locations in the world map and meet characters, talk, get items, use those items to solve puzzles.

it's a really soft and delicate feeling. i liked it!

MIHASHIRA - Steam

lots of girls. lots of bullets. big weapons.

the screen fills with bullets as you skillful switch between attack types to eliminate enemies. use your light attack to weaken enemies, and then finish them off with a heavy attack!

choose between four characters - the princess, the maid, the sister, and the miko. the four genders!

there are two weapon types - laser and solid. will your blast your enemies with beams or pummel them with a massive morningstar?

the game was really overwhelming at first. i wasn't sure if i'd be able to get used to the fast pace! but before long my reactions adjusted and i found myself making more and more progress in each of my arcade runs! defeating the first stages boss for the first time was a great feeling!

conclusion

i'm pretty cynical when it comes to new games. developers prefer to play it safe, they take popular concepts and make small changes to them. their games are boring. they don't inspire me! my heart doesn't beat! but that's not completely true! there are still exciting games out there if you look hard enough.

i don't know if these games are those, but playing them put a smile on my face and lit a fire in my heart. i'm happy i gave nextfest a fair shot this time!

there were some other games i tried...

"they are here", a first person alien horror game. it didn't run well on my computer, and i think it might use AI voices.

"changeable guardian ESTIQUE", a retro inspired shooting game that has two different versions - one for the famicom and one for the NES. the game actually came out for those console back in 2024! this is a rerelease for modern platforms. weird! unfortunately, i found it a bit dull. it didn't make my blood burn!

"夜班护士模拟器", this one doesn't seem to have an english title. you're a nurse, the hospital's haunted. i got bored very quickly. it might be good if you give it a proper shot. the translation was weird... kinda british?

"Chainsaw", an rpg with tons of style. i'm curious about the story and might give the demo a proper shot later. maybe. this was probably the best of the rest.

"Torr", a jrpg that was very gameboy-ish. the dialouge was boring. the balance seemed harsh. i dropped it quickly. it lacked personality! it was clearly inspired by dragon quest, but do they know what makes dragon quest good??

thanks for reading!
