fuzzy's top picks

Ichijouma Mankitsugurashi!

Enjoying Life in a One Tatami Room!
  • Airing: April 11th (Saturday)
  • PV: https://youtu.be/4P2dc6mphS4
  • Studio: PRA
  • Fever: ★★★★☆
  • Why I'm looking forward to it: It's a Kirara anime! Ecchi and cute, it's about working hard to protect the place you call home! Cute girls daily lives in a manga cafe!

Akane-banashi

  • Airing: April 4th (Saturday)
  • PV: https://youtu.be/yZxy7JKQhg0, https://youtu.be/PZJAOQ-7iJ8
  • Studio: Zexcs
  • Fever: ★★★★☆
  • Why I'm looking forward to it: Hard work and guts! Hikaru... surely you will have more than 30 seconds of screen time...
  • Hot Information: KKCYBER really likes the manga. So you'll watch the anime, right?

Tongari Boushi no Atelier

Witch Hat Atelier
  • Airing: April 6th (Monday)
  • PV: https://youtu.be/0BrBWvjGmWc
  • Studio: BUG FILMS
  • Fever: ★★★★☆
  • Why I'm looking forward to it: It's a wonderful story about messing up and trying hard. What's right, what's wrong stuff like that... The animation looks amazing, the voice acting sounds amazing. I'm really looking forward to this!!!

Awajima Hyakkei

One Hundred Views of Awajima
  • Airing: April 9th (Thursday)
  • PV: https://youtu.be/PRuvRsdvM8k, https://youtu.be/pVjwvAcP-8M, https://youtu.be/9OWMV9XrZ8k
  • Studio: Madhouse
  • Fever: ★★★★★
  • Why I'm looking forward to it: It's a story about wanting and trying and failing and succeeding and giving up. About pushing your dreams onto others, and having others dreams pushed onto you as well as pushing for your own dreams. It has a large cast and takes place across different eras. These characters lives all intersect around Awajima.

Kamiina Botan, Yoeru Sugata wa Yuri no Hana

  • Airing: April 10th (Friday)
  • PV: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XX90FklAWsc
  • Studio: Soigne
  • Fever: ★★★★☆
  • Why I'm looking forward to it: It's an enchanting story about girls and drinking. Really pretty. It pulls you in!
  • Hot Information: This is the second anime from Soigne, who also released Mono last year!
