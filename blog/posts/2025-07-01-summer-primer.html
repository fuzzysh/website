    Airing: July ??th
    PV: https://youtu.be/rzLiyPTwxIE

    Why I'm looking forward to it: The manga's really amazing! It's calm and thoughtful. Very charming and heartwarming. If you like "things that are good" then check it out!

    Airing: July 6th (Sunday)
    PV: https://youtu.be/ldLdilBkwVg
