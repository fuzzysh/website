Manga Time Kirara 3x3

    Hi, I'd like to write about some of my favouite manga published in Kirara! I'll present these as a 3x3 image and then get into details about each manga and why it means so much to me.

    A-Channel

    A-Channel is the ultimate high-school moe series. bb-sensei's cast is diverse and defined, with each major character falling into a clear archetype, and the expression of these archetypes is perfected. The series features cute girls wasting their precious high school years together, making good memories and forging strong bonds.

    A-Channel leans heavily into perverted jokes, and emphasises sexier charm-points like Tooru's zettai ryoiki and Yuko's curves. In fact, bb-sensei included so many jokes about boobs that his editor banned him from making any more.

    Urara Meirochou

    Urara Meirochou focuses on two main points: visual moe, meaning a physical appearence that creates moe, and character moe, meaning personality traits that endear a character to you. It pulls these off excellently, immedietely investing you into the journey of the characters and compelling you to root for them during their difficult journey.

    Hidamari Sketch

    What can I even say about Hidamari Sketch? Featuring a cast of eccentric characters, Hidamari Sketch immedietely endears them to you, and then gradually creates situations where you find yourself rooting for them.
