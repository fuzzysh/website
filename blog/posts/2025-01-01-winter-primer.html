2025 Winter Anime Season Primer

    stuff i'm looking forward to

    • Momentary Lily
      • Airing: January 2nd
      • PV: https://youtu.be/epsxyWR5psk, https://youtu.be/LJkengk1IP0
      • Studio Name: GoHands
      • Why I'm interested in it: characters seem cute and the animation in the 2nd PV is pretty nuts
    • Medalist
      • Airing: January 5th
      • PV: https://youtu.be/j3hyRR3_q1E, https://youtu.be/-Wn4DhzPS-I
      • Studio Name: ENGI
      • Why I'm interested in it: Cute little girl fighting to acheive her dreams...すき。。。
    • Flower and Asura (Hana wa Saku, Shura no Gotoku/HanaShura)
      • Airing: January 8th
      • PV: https://youtu.be/cJrnloz4m20, https://youtu.be/Hbu5KOGlihs
      • Studio Name: Studio Bind
      • Why I'm interested in it: It's a really beautiful yuri series about breaking out of your comfort zone, learning to live for yourself and chasing your dreams. The 2nd PV really blew me away. Fantastic casting decisions for the entire cast, but especially the main girls Hana and Mizuki.
    • I May Be a Guild Receptionist, but I'll Solo Any Boss to Clock Out on Time
      • Airing: January 11th
      • PV: https://youtu.be/bljeaBHners, https://youtu.be/_EnIDvUykhQ
      • Studio Name: Clover Works
      • Why I'm interested in it: I want a cute girl to kill me with a giant hammer. I trust clover works even if they're adapting something that seems like light novel slop. Rie Takahashi voices the heroine!
    • Welcome to Japan, Ms. Elf!
      • Airing: January 10th
      • PV: https://youtu.be/JpzsHob9cfc
      • Studio Name: Zero-G
      • Why I'm interested in it: I want to cohabitate with a beautiful and genki elf. The Age Of Bishoujo Has Returned.
