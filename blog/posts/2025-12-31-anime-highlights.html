2025 Anime Highlights

    Okinawa de Suki ni Natta Ko ga Hougen Sugite Tsurasugiru

    Okitsura: Fell in Love with an Okinawan Girl, but I Just Wish I Know What She's Saying
    • Studio: Millipensee
    • Why I liked it: by using a familiar romcom formula okitsura provides a platform to experience okinawan history. it's really cool and i love learning all about okinawa and falling in love with the culture alongside teruaki. it's clear how much love for okinawa is in the show. the animation is really cool. a lot of it looks like they took shortcuts but it's a very artistic thing. they're taking advantage of animation as an artistic medium to make their jobs easier is how it feels. it's really great to watch anime that puts animation as art in center stage. the show uses the characters moe to rope you in and help you experience okinawan culture. even the grandmas are moe. i love moe.
